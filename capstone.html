for Trucking Services — Improving financial and administrative operations through digital transformation.

2025 Capstone Project Best IT Capstone Nominee

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing a Web-Based Record Management and Dispatching System for Trucking Services to improve financial and administrative operations. The system is designed to semi-automate salary invoicing, expense tracking, route optimization, and dispatch coordination, replacing outdated manual processes that contribute to inefficiencies and errors.

Built with modern web technologies, the system offers an intuitive interface for dispatchers, drivers, and administrators to manage loads, track deliveries, and generate status reports efficiently.

Status: Completed & Deployed

Problem Statement

The Philippine Trucking Industry continues to face significant challenges due to its heavy reliance on outdated manual processes, particularly in financial and administrative operations. Many trucking companies still manage their invoicing through paper-based records or excel spreadsheets, requiring manual input for every trip

Project Objectives

  • Automate dispatching and load assignment
  • Provide real-time record management for transactions
  • Generate automated Status reports and summaries
  • Improve communication between dispatchers and drivers
  • Reduce operational costs through digital transformation
